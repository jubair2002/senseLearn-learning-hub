Core Capabilities

Designed for Distinct Needs

We don't believe in "one size fits all." Our platform morphs to the user's specific disability profile.

Visual to Audio Translation

For visually impaired users, all on-screen content is instantly converted to high-fidelity audio. Graphs are described via AI narrative.

  • ✓ Screen Reader Friendly
  • ✓ Braille Display Support

Sign Language Integration

Every video lecture comes with a dedicated sign language interpreter window (PIP) and synchronized closed captions with speaker ID.

  • ✓ Real-time Captioning
  • ✓ Visual Alert Systems

AAC & Symbol Communication

For users with speech impairments, we integrate Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) boards directly into the chat.

  • ✓ Text-to-Speech Engine
  • ✓ Predictive Symbol Typing
